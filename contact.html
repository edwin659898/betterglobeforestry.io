About better Globe Forestry

Better Globe Forestry (BGF) Ltd was incorporated in Kenya in 2004 and in Uganda in 2009 with the vision To eradicate Poverty in Africa through forestry developments. Its mission, of BGF is by implementing own forestry projects, and in the name of clients, to plant as many trees as there are people on this planet, and thereby finance a sustainable implementation of the Vision.
