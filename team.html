Jean-Paul Deprins

Managing Director

Jean-Paul, a Belgian national, has served as the Managing Director of Better Globe Forestry Limited in East Africa for two decades. With extensive experience in senior management roles across Europe and East Africa, Jean-Paul is a strong advocate for ethical and transparent business practices, emphasizing a methodical and evidence-based approach to tackling challenges.

Jan Vandenabeele

Senior Director Forestry

Jan is a Belgian forester and agronomist with 40 years of experience in management and technical implementation of locally and internationally financed projects in Europe, Africa, South America, and Asia. As a senior director in BGF his wealth of experience and his high level contacts in the forestry world are...

invaluable and have opened many doors such allowing us to be the pioneers in dryland afforestation in East Africa. As the Editor-in-chief of Miti, he is the driving force behind the magazine with editorial boards in Kenya, Uganda, and Tanzania.


Lawrence Mulandi

Finance and Operations Director

Lawrence started working with Better Globe Forestry (BGF) as an accountant, became a finance manager and was later promoted to Finance Director. He has been instrumental in guiding the accounts department through the...

transition from a small company to a fast-growing medium-sized organisation. He holds a master's degree in business administration - strategic management and is now pursuing a PhD in business administration, specialising in finance. He is also a certified public accountant of Kenya - CPA (K) - and a certified internal systems auditor. Lawrence is an observer to the board.


Samuel Nakhone

Executive Director Forestry

Samuel is an experienced forester with a BSc Forestry degree from Moi University and 15 years of work experience, managing teams. He is polished in natural resource management and environmental conservation ...

with an inclination to social enterprise, sustainable community participatory environmental management, and an interest in climate change. He started his professional career with the company and rose through the ranks. He is a driven cross-functional team leader leveraging the above-mentioned competencies to establish competent, motivated, and result-oriented teams to achieve both organisational and personal goals within stipulated timeframes and cost. Samuel is an observer to the board.


Lydia Kathure Kubai

Deputy Director Human Resources

Lydia joined Better Globe Forestry in the year 2018 as a Senior Accountant. She is now in charge of the Human Resources department. She is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA) with over 10 years working experience in the private sector, currently finalizing her bachelor's degree in Commerce, Finance Option. Lydia is a Member of the Institute of Certified Public Accounts of Kenya.

Claudiah Caroline Deprins

Deputy Director Communications

Claudiah joined the Better Globe family in 2011 as Partnerships Coordinator working in Child Africa. In 2018 she started working with BGF's Publication Miti Magazine as the Subscription's Coordinator. With over 10 years experience in Public Relations, she became BGF's Deputy Director of Communications in 2022.

About better Globe Forestry

Better Globe Forestry (BGF) Ltd was incorporated in Kenya in 2004 and in Uganda in 2009 with the vision To eradicate Poverty in Africa through forestry developments. Its mission, of BGF is by implementing own forestry projects, and in the name of clients, to plant as many trees as there are people on this planet, and thereby finance a sustainable implementation of the Vision.
